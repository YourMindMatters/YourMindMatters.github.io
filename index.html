It’s time to destigmatise mental health altogether!


The Your Mind Matters Project

The Lift Off Project

Your Mind Matters is a NCS-Graduate-run campaign raising awareness of mental illness and wellbeing. This campaign derived from the Lift Off mental health awareness campaign launched in 2016, and our project is extending the message of raising awareness of mental illnesses, destigmatising them and also spreading information about local services in the Reading area people may not know about. Our objective is to spread our message to as many people as possible to insure they don’t have to suffer alone, or for them to be able to tell people they know about services they can access to help themselves in the Reading area.


Many of us suffer with mental health problems at some stage of our life, and the cause can be down to anything (no matter how small or big it is). But many people who suffer aren’t aware of facilities in their area to be able to aid them. Many websites like ‘youngminds.org.uk’ and ‘mind.org.uk’ exist to give advice on different mental disorders as well as topics like self harm and suicidal thoughts. They also provide more advice on how to care for yourself when you’re going through these problems. If talk therapy is something you may require, No.5 Counselling in Reading offers counselling to young people (10-25) and adults (25+), offering 20 free counselling sessions. You may only need one or two to get you back on the right path, but they believe that offering twenty in total can insure problems are kept under control and don’t come back—and you’re always welcome back!

For more information on where to get help visit:

- Youngminds.org.uk - Mind.org.uk - No5.org.uk
